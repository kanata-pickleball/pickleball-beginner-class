5. Mini gameplay – 60 min

# Week 1 – Introduction & Fundamentals

## Goal
Understand the game and start doing some game.

## Topics
- Introduction to pickleball
- Court layout and basic rules
- Grip and ready position
- Forehand groundstroke

## Drills
- Paddle control: bounce ball on paddle
- Forehand partner rally (short court)

## Game
 - Two bounce rules
 - Scoring details
